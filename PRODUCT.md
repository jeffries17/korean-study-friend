# Gongbu Buddy — Product Vision

## What we are

**The fastest path from "I saw that word" to "I own that word."**

You're watching a drama, reading a menu, getting a text. You screenshot it.
Gongbu Buddy extracts the vocab, builds the cards, and brings them back to you
at exactly the right moment to make them stick — using spaced repetition.

Your Korean, from your real life. Not ours.

---

## Who we're for

Someone who is **already encountering Korean** and wants to turn those
encounters into lasting vocabulary. Not a complete beginner starting from zero.
Not someone who wants a structured curriculum.

---

## What we are not

| Not us | Who does it |
|---|---|
| Grammar lessons | Talk To Me In Korean, Pimsleur |
| Structured curriculum / levels | Duolingo, Rosetta Stone |
| Conversation practice | iTalki, Pimsleur, HelloTalk |
| Podcast / video content | TTMIK, KoreanClass101 |
| Dictionary / translation lookup | Naver, Papago |

---

## The feature test

Before adding anything, ask:

> **"Does this help a word from real life stick better?"**

- Yes → consider it
- No → not our job

---

## Core loop (never break this)

```
See Korean in the wild
      ↓
Screenshot → AI extracts vocab
      ↓
Cards saved to your deck
      ↓
SRS brings them back at the right time
      ↓
You own the word
```

Every feature should accelerate or strengthen one step in this loop.

---

## What "best" looks like for us

- Extraction is fast, accurate, and handles messy real-world text
- Cards feel personal — the example sentence sounds like something a person says
- Review is frictionless — 2 minutes on your phone, then done
- The deck grows naturally from your life, not from a word list someone else made

---

## Guardrails

- **No built-in word lists** — if we add curated content, we become a curriculum app
- **No social features** — leaderboards, friend streaks, sharing decks push us toward Duolingo
- **No grammar teaching** — out of scope, full stop
- **Keep the UI minimal** — every screen should have one obvious thing to do
